Which food delivery app is cheapest?

It varies by location, restaurant, and dish. GrubHub, Uber Eats, and DoorDash all price differently — and delivery fees change constantly. The only way to know for sure is to compare them in real time, which is exactly what EatMe does.

How does EatMe compare delivery prices?

EatMe scrapes live menus from GrubHub, Uber Eats, and DoorDash simultaneously. It calculates the true total cost — menu price plus delivery fee — for every matching item at every nearby restaurant, then ranks them cheapest first.

Is EatMe free to use?

Yes. EatMe is completely free. No account, no subscription, no fees. We just help you find the cheapest delivery option.
