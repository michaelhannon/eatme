Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

EatMe ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our food price comparison service at www.eatme.tech.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service:

  • Search queries — the food items you search for
  • Delivery address — used solely to retrieve local pricing and restaurant availability from delivery platforms
  • Usage data — standard server logs including IP address, browser type, and pages visited

We do not require account registration and do not collect your name, email address, or payment information.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Fetch real-time pricing from food delivery platforms on your behalf
  • Display and compare results in your browser
  • Improve the performance and reliability of our service
  • Monitor for abuse or technical issues

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Address Data

Your delivery address is transmitted to our server to query local restaurant pricing. We do not permanently store your address. Address data is used only for the duration of your search session and is not linked to any persistent user profile.

Cookies and Local Storage

We may use browser local storage to remember your most recent search address for convenience. No tracking cookies are set. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

Third-Party Services

EatMe retrieves pricing data from third-party delivery platforms including DoorDash, Grubhub, Seamless, and Uber Eats. Your address is used to query these platforms on your behalf. Please review their respective privacy policies for information on how they handle data.

We may use infrastructure services such as Railway for hosting. These providers may process technical data (such as IP addresses) in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data Retention

Server logs are retained for a limited period for debugging and security purposes and are not used for profiling or marketing. Search queries are not stored in a persistent database tied to your identity.

Children's Privacy

EatMe is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact page.

EatMe is an independent price comparison tool and is not affiliated with DoorDash, Grubhub, Seamless, or Uber Eats.
